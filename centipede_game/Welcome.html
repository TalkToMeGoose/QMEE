how it works

You and the other participants will be divided into groups of two. Each group will play several rounds of a game where each turn you are able to take or pass. If you take the game ends, and you receive the payoffs listed for that turn of the game. However, if you pass, the other player then has the option to take or pass it back to you. Each time the game is passed, the possible payout increases.

Beware: there are two different sized payoff piles. One large and one small. When it is your turn, the payoff you receive will always be the large pile while your partner receives the small pile. If it is your partners turn, then you will receive the smaller pile while they receive the large pile. Please make sure to pay attention to the payoffs each round.
